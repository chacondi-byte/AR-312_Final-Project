This is a personal collection of my favorite PS4 and PS5 video games. I created this site to organize the games I enjoy the most, track the hours I’ve put into them, and highlight the titles that stand out to me.

Each game in this collection is ranked, listed, and sorted in a way that makes it easy to explore. Whether it’s action, story-driven adventures, RPGs, or competitive titles, every game here is something I’ve spent time with and genuinely enjoyed.

This site isn’t just a list — it’s a snapshot of my gaming taste.
